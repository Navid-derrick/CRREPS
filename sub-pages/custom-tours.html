CUSTOM TOURS

What is a “custom tour”?

A custom tour is a vacation package that includes the infrastructure of a trip (accommodations, transportation, day tours & activities, and guides where applicable) that’s been designed based on your specific preferences and interests. Every day and detail is tailored to you.

Benefits of choosing a custom tour:

  • Flexibility

    Flexibility has therefore become a must-have for both business travelers and leisure travelers seeking a much-needed getaway from the stresses of the current reality. Restrictions are continuously tightened and loosened as the world rolls out vaccination drives while simultaneously dealing with newer mutations of the virus such as the delta variant. In response to this, we have adapted our policies to make it possible for travelers to alter or cancel their bookings for free or for a small fee.

  • Value

    If vacation is an escape from real life, then we propose that the real value of travel is about much more than taking vacation. Custom tours can be modified in any way they need to fit your budget. Self-drives and staying in smaller hotels and B&B’s can pare down cost if higher-end accommodations and chauffeurs are too much.

  • No compromises!

    Each day’s tour and activity is selected based on your interests. Love horseback riding and not interested in museums? Your tour will reflect that. Are you a history buff and want some outdoor adventures? Your personalized itinerary will incorporate that!

  • If you want luxury, we can kick it up a notch!

    Especially for honeymooners or those looking to indulge themselves for fun, creating a custom tour allows us to upgrade your accommodations, pre-select private tours, and arrange private chauffeured transportation for a little (or a lot!) of glam on your vacation.
